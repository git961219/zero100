**[Why External GPS?]**

Your phone GPS updates once per second (1Hz).
The u-blox M9 module updates 25 times per second (25Hz).

- Phone GPS: error of +/- 0.5 seconds or more
- External 25Hz GPS: error of +/- 0.04 seconds

This is the same technology used by professional tools like VBOX and Dragy.

---

**[How to Use]**

1. Connect a u-blox M9 GPS module to your phone via USB OTG
2. Open Zero100 and wait for GPS lock (about 30 seconds)
3. Select your mode: 0-100 / custom interval / lap timer
4. Start driving - measurement begins and ends automatically
5. Review your results and save

**[FAQ]**

Q. Can I use my phone's built-in GPS?
A. Yes, but external GPS is strongly recommended for accurate results.

Q. How does this compare to Dragy?
A. Dragy requires a dedicated Bluetooth device ($200). Zero100 works with a standard u-blox module ($30-50) and delivers comparable precision.

Q. What is 1-foot rollout?
A. An NHRA drag racing standard. The timer starts after your car moves 1 foot (30.48cm), removing launch control reaction time for a fair comparison.

Q. What vehicles are supported?
A. Any vehicle. Cars, motorcycles, karts - if GPS signal is available, Zero100 can measure it.
